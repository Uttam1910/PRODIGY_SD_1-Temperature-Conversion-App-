About Temperature Scales

Temperature is measured using different scales. Here's a quick guide:

  • Celsius (°C): Used worldwide, except for the US, for most temperature measurements.
  • Fahrenheit (°F): Primarily used in the US for weather, cooking, and some industrial purposes.
  • Kelvin (K): The scientific temperature scale used in physics and chemistry.
